# Business Requirements

## Project Context

The sales leadership team wants to improve visibility into revenue, customers, products, sales representatives, and commercial opportunities.

Currently, CRM data is not modeled for analytics. The goal is to create a reliable analytics layer that supports reporting and decision-making.

## Business Users

The main users are:

- Sales Director
- Sales Managers
- Regional Managers
- Finance Team
- Marketing Team

## Business Questions

### Revenue

- What is the total revenue?
- How does revenue evolve month over month?
- Which regions generate the most revenue?
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which customers generate the most revenue?

### Sales Performance

- Which sales representatives perform best?
- What is the average order value by sales representative?
- Which regions are underperforming?

### Customer Analysis

- Who are the top customers?
- Which customers are inactive?
- What is the customer lifetime value?

### Opportunity Analysis

- What is the total pipeline value?
- What is the opportunity conversion rate?
- Which sales representatives convert the most opportunities?

## Success Criteria

The project is successful if it provides:

- Clean and documented data models
- Reliable business KPIs
- Tested dbt models
- A Power BI dashboard
- Clear project documentation
